Introducing the SwaddleDesigns Cozy Plush Sleeping Sack

Perfect for babies leaving the swaddling stage, this cozy sleeping sack offers a safe and comfortable transition. Made from ultra-soft microfiber fabric, it creates a snug, secure feeling while giving your little one the freedom to move and explore.

The 2-way zipper design makes it easy to dress, undress, and adjust the fit as needed, so you can keep your baby comfortable throughout the night. With a TOG rating of 2.2, it's ideal for mild temperatures between 65 and 70 degrees Fahrenheit, ensuring a restful sleep environment.

Its lightweight and breathable construction makes it suitable for use all year round. Available in three sizes for newborns up to toddlers, this sleeping sack is designed to grow with your baby. Plus, it blends seamlessly with our range of baby essentials, offering a stylish and cohesive look for your nursery.

Whether it's bedtime or a quiet nap in the day, the SwaddleDesigns Cozy Plush Sleeping Sack is a must-have for parents who want their baby to feel safe, cozy, and free to move.
