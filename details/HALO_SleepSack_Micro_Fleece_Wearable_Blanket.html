HALO SleepSack: Safe & Cozy Sleep for Your Baby

Designed with safety and comfort in mind, the HALO SleepSack is the perfect transition from swaddling to independent sleep. It’s a safe alternative to traditional blankets, eliminating the risk of suffocation and ensuring a peaceful night for your little one.

Ultra-Soft Fabric for Gentle Warmth

Crafted from ultra-soft micro-fleece minky dot fabric, the HALO SleepSack provides ideal thermal comfort. With a TOG 1.0 rating, it keeps your baby warm during cooler seasons or in colder climates, without the hassle of loose blankets.

Promotes Healthy Hip Development

The sack design allows for natural hip movement, supporting healthy development. This feature is recognized by the International Hip Dysplia Institute, making the HALO SleepSack a trusted choice for parents who value both comfort and safety.

Easy Diaper Access with Inverted Zip

The inverted zipper makes it simple to change diapers without waking your baby. No more fumbling in the dark—this design ensures your little one stays comfortable and secure throughout the night.

Designed for Optimal Temperature Control

With a sleeveless cut, the HALO SleepSack prevents overheating and ensures a proper fit. This breathable design helps maintain the right temperature, promoting better sleep and health for your baby.

Why Parents Choose HALO SleepSack

As a safer sleep solution, the HALO SleepSack offers peace of mind. It is loved by hospital nurseries worldwide and is a must-have for parents who want to ensure their baby sleeps safely, comfortably, and soundly.
