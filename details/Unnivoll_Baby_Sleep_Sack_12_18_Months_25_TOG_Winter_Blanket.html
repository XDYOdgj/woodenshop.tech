Unnivoll Baby Sleep Sack

A cozy and safe sleep solution designed for your little one. Made from 100% cotton, this ultra-soft sleep sack is gentle on your baby's delicate skin and ensures a comfortable sleep experience throughout the night.

Why Choose the Unnivoll Sleep Sack?

With a 2.5 TOG insulation rating, the Unnivoll sleep sack is perfect for colder weather and cooler room temperatures, helping to keep your baby warm and snug during winter months. It's an essential item for parents who value both comfort and security.

The sleep sack is safety-certified by the Consumer Product Safety Commission, offering a secure and breathable environment that allows natural movement while preventing the baby from kicking off the blanket. Its quilted stitching ensures that the filler remains evenly distributed, avoiding lumps and shifting for a smooth, comfortable fit.

Featuring a 2-way zipper, this sleep sack makes changing diapers or dressing your baby simple and stress-free without interrupting their rest. The design is ideal for toddlers aged 12-18 months, providing a snug fit while still allowing freedom of movement.

Versatile and Durable

Available in a wide range of sizes from S to XL, the Unnivoll baby sleep sack is a great choice for both boys and girls. The breathable cotton fabric ensures your baby stays comfortable all night, while the zippered closure allows for quick and easy access when needed.

Whether you're looking for a reliable sleep aid for daily use or a special gift for a new baby, the Unnivoll sleep sack delivers value, style, and peace of mind.
